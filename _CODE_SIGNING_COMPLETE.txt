🧪 VERIFY SIGNATURE
================================================================================

Windows UI:
   Chuột phải .exe → Properties → Digital Signatures

Expected:
   ✅ SignerCertificate: CN=OCR License System
   ✅ Status: Valid (hoặc UnknownError cho self-signed - OK!)
   ✅ Timestamp: DigiCert Timestamp

================================================================================
❓ TROUBLESHOOTING
================================================================================

Issue: "Cannot create certificate"
Fix:   Chạy PowerShell với quyền Administrator

Issue: "Set-AuthenticodeSignature failed"
Fix:   .\sign_exe.ps1 -ExePath "app.exe" -PfxFile "OCR_CodeSigning.pfx"

Issue: Certificate expired
Fix:   .\create_self_signed_cert.ps1 (tạo mới)

Issue: Vẫn bị cảnh báo virus
Fix:   Upload lên VirusTotal, share link scan với users
